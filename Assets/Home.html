What we do...

This is an ongoing prototype of a Social and Emotional application designed to track, monitor, and regulate emotions in the context of e-learning.

Social and emotional regulation has become an important part of learning in the context of attention and emotional tracking. Attention or emotional dysregulation has been linked to poor academic outcomes, misbehavior, and task confidence.

E-Profile contains different applications intended to capture changes in emotions, changes in levels of anxiety, for the purpose of both self regulation and curriculum management.

The Following is the E-Profile Framework, which is intended to be site agnostic so that social and emotional learning can be incorporated into online, hybrid, or in person curriculums.
