Emotion Tracker

First Quadrant Represents High Intensity Postive Emotions. These are emotions like euphoria.

Second Quadrant Represents Low Intensity Positive Emotions. These are emotions like content.

Third Quadrant Represents Low Intensity Negative Emotions. These are emotions like frustration.

Fourth Quadrant Represents High Intensity Negative Emotions. These are emotions like rage.
